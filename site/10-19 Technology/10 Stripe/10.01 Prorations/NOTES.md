# Notes

Working notes and learner preferences for this workspace.

## Learner preferences
- **Lens:** product/finance, not engineering. Lead with money and invoice line items; use API names only as labels.
- **Examples:** concrete dollar figures over abstract formulas.

## Curriculum roadmap
1. `0001-what-is-proration` — core mechanics (credit + charge, proration date, line items). ← first lesson
2. `0002-proration-behavior` — `create_prorations` vs `none` vs `always_invoice`.
3. `0003-previewing-the-bill` — forecasting the proration before applying a change.
4. `0004-edge-cases` — trials, billing-cycle anchors, multiple changes per period, negative invoices / credit balance.

Teach one at a time, gated on the learner's questions and demonstrated understanding.
